Aloha Chicken Pizza (L1)

Pizza.... Perfect for the lunch!

45min Time
6 Serving
Preparation - Medium
266 kcal/Serving

Ingredients:

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

  • 4 tablespoons Mayonnaise
  • 4 tablespoons Thousand Island Salad Dressing
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 50 g yellow onion, diced
  • 200 g skinless chicken breast, thinly sliced
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ teaspoon white pepper powder
  • 2 pieces of 10” thin pizza crusts, ready-made
  • 100 g canned pineapple, diced
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese

The steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan and sauté the yellow onion until soft, then add the chicken and fry until cooked. Season with salt and white pepper powder. Let it cool.
  2. In a bowl, combine Lady's Choice Real Mayonnaise and Lady's Choice Thousand Island Salad Dressing.
  3. Spread the salad dressing mixture evenly on the pizza crust and arrange the chicken and pineapple mixture on top.
  4. Sprinkle a little mozzarella cheese on top of the pizza and bake in a preheated oven at 170°C for 20 minutes or until the cheese melts.
  5. Garnish with parsley and black pepper and serve hot.
