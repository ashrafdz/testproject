Lemak Cili Api Crab (L3)

Hot Meal..... Perfect for family lunch!

45min Time
6 Serving
Preparation - Medium
266 kcal/Serving

Ingredients:

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

  • 5 blue crabs, cleaned and drained
  • 150 ml coconut milk
  • 1 stalk lemongrass, bruised
  • 1 piece tamarind slice
  • 1 turmeric leaf, sliced
  • ¼ teaspoon sugar
  • 1 Anchovy Stock Cube
  • Blended Ingredients:
  • 5 bird's eye chilies
  • 5 shallots
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 inch fresh turmeric
  • ½ cup water

The steps:

  1. Combine the blended ingredients and water in a pot and bring to a boil.
  2. Then, add the coconut milk and stir until well combined. Add the crabs and other ingredients. Season with Knorr Anchovy Stock Cube.
  3. Cook for 7-9 minutes and the crab curry is ready to be served.
