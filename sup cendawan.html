Western-style Creamy Onion and Mushroom Soup (D1)

Creamy Soup.... Perfect for the dinner!

30min Time
4 Serving
Preparation - Easy
106 kcal/Serving

Ingredients:

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

    • 1 Vegetable Stock Cube (6 Cubes 60g)
    • 40 g unsalted butter
    • 250 g yellow onions, sliced
    • 3 cloves garlic, roughly chopped
    • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
    • 250 g portobello mushrooms, roughly chopped
    • 100 g white button mushrooms, roughly chopped
    • 3 cups water
    • 1 cup milk
    • ¼ teaspoon salt
    • ¼ teaspoon black pepper powder
    • ¼ cup cooking cream

The steps:

  1. Heat the butter in a pot and sauté the yellow onions and garlic until fragrant. Add the fresh thyme and all the mushrooms. Sauté for a few minutes.
  2. Then, pour in the water and bring to a boil. Simmer for 5-6 minutes. Turn off the heat and let cool.
  3. Once cooled, remove the thyme sprigs and pour the soup mixture into a food processor and blend into a smooth puree.
  4. Next, pour the blended soup back into the pot and add the milk, Knorr Vegetable Stock Cube, salt, and black pepper powder. Bring to a boil.
  5. Finally, add the cream and stir until well combined. Serve hot.
