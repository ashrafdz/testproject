Spaghetti Chicken Black Pepper (D2)

Best Spaghettin Ever!

30min Time
3 Serving
Preparation - Easy
55 kcal/Serving

Ingredients:

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

    • 1 Chicken Stock Cube, crushed
    • 3 tablespoons cooking oil
    • 1 large onion, sliced
    • 20 g garlic, sliced
    • 30 g ginger, sliced
    • 250 g chicken, sliced
    • 100 ml water
    • 1 tablespoon light soy sauce
    • 1 tablespoon dark soy sauce
    • 100 g yellow bell pepper, sliced
    • 100 g red bell pepper, sliced
    • 30 g black pepper powder
    • 450 g spaghetti, cooked
    • A handful of coriander leaves for garnish

The steps:

  1. Heat the oil in a pan, saut√© the onions, garlic, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the chicken and black pepper powder. Fry for one minute.
  3. Add water, light soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and the Knorr Chicken Stock Cube. Stir until well combined and add the bell peppers.
  4. Finally, add the cooked spaghetti and mix well. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
