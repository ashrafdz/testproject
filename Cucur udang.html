Shrimp fritters (D3)

Relax Snack!

25min Time
6 Serving
Preparation - Easy
80 kcal/Serving

Ingredients:

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

    • 200 grams self-raising flour
    • 1 cup water
    • ½ onion, chopped
    • 1 red chili, thinly sliced
    • 3 stalks chives, cut into 1-inch lengths
    • ½ cup cooked corn kernels
    • 100 grams small shrimp, peeled
    • ½ tbsp Knorr All-in-One Seasoning
    • Oil for frying

The steps:

  1. Mix flour, Knorr All-in-One Seasoning, and water. Stir until well combined. Ensure the mixture is not too watery.
  2. Add the onion, chili, chives, and corn kernels, and mix well.
  3. Heat cooking oil in a pan. Pour the batter into a heated ladle and place shrimp on top of the batter. Fry in the oil.
  4. Shake the fritter mixture until it detaches from the ladle.
  5. Fry the fritters until golden brown and drain the oil.
  6. Serve hot.
