Porridge (B3)

Porridge only takes 20 minutes to prepare. Perfect for the morning!

20min Time
1 Serving
Preparation - Easy
158 kcal/Serving

Ingredients:

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 8 cups water
  • 200 g chicken, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch ginger, thinly sliced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon white pepper
  • 2 tablespoons cooking oil
  • 2 boiled eggs, halved
  • Green onions, sliced
  • Cilantro, chopped
  • Fried shallots, for garnish

The steps:

  1. Wash the rice and drain.
  2. Heat the oil in a large pot. Sauté the garlic and ginger until fragrant.
  3. Add the sliced chicken and cook until the chicken changes color.
  4. Add the washed rice to the pot and stir well.
  5. Add water and bring to a boil.
  6. Lower the heat and let the porridge cook until the rice breaks down and the porridge thickens, about 30 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  7. Add salt and white pepper, adjust the seasoning to taste.
  8. Serve the porridge in bowls. Top with boiled eggs, green onions, cilantro, and fried shallots for garnish.
