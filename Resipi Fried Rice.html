Kampung Fried Rice (B1)

Nasi Goreng Kampung only takes 15 minutes to prepare. Perfect for those in a rush in the morning!

20min Time
1 Serving
Preparation - Easy
460 kcal/Serving

Ingredients:

Ingredients needed for this recipe:

  • 4 cups of cold rice
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 small shallots
  • 6-8 stalks of chili rice
  • 2 tbsp anchovies
  • 1 piece of chicken filling
  • 1 bunch of kale
  • 1 inch belacan
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1-2 tsp sweet soy sauce
  • 1-2 tbsp sambal belacan
  • 1 cup anchovies
  • Cucumber
  • Red chillies

The steps:

  1. Kale leaves, cut and separate stems and leaves.
  2. Remove the fat and bones from the chicken and then cut it into small pieces.
  3. Roughly chop small shallots and garlic.
  4. Coarsely chop the rice chili.
  5. Finely mash anchovies, garlic, shallots and rice chili.
  6. Heat the oil and fry the eggs. Stir to break up. Remove and set aside.
  7. Fry anchovies until crisp. Remove and set aside.
  8. Saute the mashed ingredients until dry.
  9. Add grilled belacan and chicken filling. Stir until the chicken is half cooked.
  10. Add the spinach and sambal belacan (add more belacan and chili rice if you don't want to add sambal belacan).
  11. Add cold rice and stir well.
  12. Add salt, eggs, kale leaves, sweetened condensed soy sauce and fried anchovies (keep some for sprinkling at the end). Stir well.
  13. Serve with fried anchovies, cucumber slices and red chilies.
